Welcome to the Snake game! I believe that nobody would not know about this game, but I will make a short description of this game...
In this game, you as the snake needs to eat the required amount of foods within the time frame allocated while avoiding bumping into the obstacles and the snake itself. 

================
Controls:
    Arrow keys - up: Moves up
    Arrow keys - down: Moves down
    Arrow keys - left: Moves left
    Arrow keys - right: Moves right



Now, please enjoy the game...
